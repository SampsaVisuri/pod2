How To Play

Approaching game

Choose a place with enough space to play. Be ware of your surroundings! Choose a distance you want to throw from (40m, 60m or 80m).

Measure the distance with your feet from the basket (one meter is approximately one large step forward).

Throw 10 discs at the basket.

You get scored for each disc that is inside circle one (10 meter in radius). You will get a higher score if your disc lands 5 meters or closer from the basket. Hitting the basket will give you higher a score than landing inside the 5 meter radius. Throwing the disc inside the basket will give you the highest possible score.
